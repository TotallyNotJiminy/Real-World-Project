About

The Student food App was created by students for students. We know that coming up with meal plans is difficult, especially when you’re living on a budget. To make your life easier, we take your food preferences, budget, and lifestyle and create recipes that best suit you. We compare ingredient prices for the all major supermarkets and tell you the cheapest option. But if that’s still too much hassle for you, we can deliver the exact ingredients for your chosen weekly recipes in a convenient cardboard box. One package, one delivery with a weeks’ worth of meals, easy to prepare, easy to cook and crafted for you.
